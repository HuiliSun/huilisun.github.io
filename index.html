I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Biomedical Engineering working with Dr.Dustin Scheinost in Yale. My research aims to understand brain development trajectory during infancy and predict brain maturation and individual behaviors in a later age. Combining multi-modality neuroimaging tools including dMRI, fMRI and EEG, I commit to develop methods based on machine learning and brain modeling to generate predictive models for cognitive performance and neurodevelopmental risks in early childhood. Before joining MINDS lab, I worked with Dr.Shi Gu in UESTC on the detection of brain dynamic community structure.
